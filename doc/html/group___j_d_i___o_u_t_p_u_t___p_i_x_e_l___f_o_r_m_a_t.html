enum JDI_OUTPUT_PIXEL_FORMAT
Enumerator
JDI_OUT_1BIT 

If any of the highest bits of any color channel of RGB input is 1, the output pixel is represented as 1; Otherwise, the output pixel is represented as 0.

JDI_OUT_3BIT 

R1(bit 5) G1(bit 4) B1(bit 3) R0(bit 2) G0(bit 1) B0(bit 0).

JDI_OUT_4BIT 

R0(bit 3) G0(bit 2) B0(bit 1) dummy(bit 0).
