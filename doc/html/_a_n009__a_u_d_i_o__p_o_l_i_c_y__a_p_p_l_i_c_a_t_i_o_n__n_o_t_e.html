1 Introduction

The purpose of this application note is to give an overview of Audio policy, by this module, you can:

  • Get/set the bud stream status, for example: idle, in audio or in voice
  • Get/set the audio track latency, volume
  • Get/set the mic/speaker mute status and do mic switch
  • Handle the bud location change event(in/out ear, in/out case, a2dp pause/resume, voice tranfer to BUD or AG)
  • Handle the avrcp and a2dp status change event, and set to global app_db.a2dp_play_status/app_db.avrcp_play_status
  • Play/stop tone or VP
