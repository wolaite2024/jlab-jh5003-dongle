OTA Over The Air

1 RWS OTA

A demo project on how to integrate OTA feature of single bud into user application is provided in OTA User Manual . The RTL87X3E and RTL87X3D support to update two buds one time automatically for RWS Headset via both BLE or SPP. What's more, users can still choose to update one by one by disconnect the two buds.
