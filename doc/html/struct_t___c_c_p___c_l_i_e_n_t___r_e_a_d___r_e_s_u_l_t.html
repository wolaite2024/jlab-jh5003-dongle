◆ bearer_signal_strength

uint8_t bearer_signal_strength

Level of the signal of the telephone bearer.

  • 0 : No service.
  • 1 to 99 : Meaning of the values is left up to the implementation.
  • 100 : Maximum signal strength.
  • 101 to 254: Reserved for future use.
  • 255 : Signal strength is unavailable or has no meaning for this particular bearer.

◆ bearer_signal_strength_reporting_interval

uint8_t bearer_signal_strength_reporting_interval

Reporting interval expressed in seconds.

  • 0 : Signal strength is reported as soon as the signal strength changes.
  • 1 to 255.
