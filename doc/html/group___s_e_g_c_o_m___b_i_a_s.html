enum SEGCOM_BIAS
Enumerator
BIAS_STATIC 

No bias voltage.

BIAS_1_2 

A bias of 1/2 means that the voltage of the common electrode is at the midpoint of the drive voltage.

BIAS_1_3 

A bias of 1/3 means that the voltage of the common electrode is 1/3 and 2/3 of the drive voltage.

BIAS_RSVD 

Reserved.
