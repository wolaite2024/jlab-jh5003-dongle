Detailed Description

Enable and disable Active Noise Cancellation (ANC).

Active Noise Cancellation (ANC), or Active Noise Reduction (ANR), uses noise-cancelling speakers to reduce unwanted background noise. ANC will help you get away from the noise world when enjoying music or just quiet your mind. While enabling ANC will consume more battery power.
