Detailed Description

Define the bitmask of Audio Stream out/sink and in/source devices.

The low 16 bits represent audio out devices, and high 16 bits represent audio in devices.

Note
If the Audio Stream chooses AUDIO_DEVICE_OUT_DEFAULT or AUDIO_DEVICE_IN_DEFAULT, it will be dynamically routed to the most relevant hardware device in different audio scenarios.
