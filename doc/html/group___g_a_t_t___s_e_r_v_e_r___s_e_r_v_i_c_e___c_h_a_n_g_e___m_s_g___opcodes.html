◆ GATT_SERVER_SERVICE_CHANGE_SET_SERVICE_CHANGE_STATE_IND

#define GATT_SERVER_SERVICE_CHANGE_SET_SERVICE_CHANGE_STATE_IND   0x0001
  • Inform APP to handle and save service change state. Usage refer to service_change and service_change_state in T_GATT_SERVER_SERVICE_CHANGE_SET_SERVICE_CHANGE_STATE_IND

◆ GATT_SERVER_SERVICE_CHANGE_SET_DATABASE_HASH_INFO

◆ GATT_SERVER_SERVICE_CHANGE_GET_SERVICE_CHANGE_STATE_IND

#define GATT_SERVER_SERVICE_CHANGE_GET_SERVICE_CHANGE_STATE_IND   0x0020
  • Inform APP to supply service change state by gatt_server_service_change_update_service_change_state If device is bonded, service change state is previous state that is saved by APP. If device is not bonded, service change state is 0 .

◆ GATT_SERVER_SERVICE_CHANGE_GET_DATABASE_HASH_IND
