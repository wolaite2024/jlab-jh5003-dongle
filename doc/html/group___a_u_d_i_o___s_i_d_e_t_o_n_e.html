Detailed Description

Control and customize the sidetone engine in the audio framework.

Sidetone is the sound of the user voice captured by the microphone that is fed back into the earpiece. Sidetone gives indications to the user that the microphone is picking up the voice; the voice is loud enough or too low; and the voice connection is established or dropped.
